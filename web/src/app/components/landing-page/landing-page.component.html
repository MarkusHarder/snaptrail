Capturing journeys, one photo at a time


Why Snaptrail?

This Webapp servers two purposes: First to document my journey as a photographer. Taking pictures has been a lot of fun for me. Getting out of the house and exploring nature still feels great after a long day in front of the monitor. But going through pictures is a bit tedious, and at times plain boring. The goal of Snaptrail is to have a bit more motivation to do so! It might even showcase improvement over time! Secondly it serves as a technical demo, showcasing my skills and knowledge as a software engineer. skills as a software engineer.

About

This Application has been implemented using mostly things I already know - but you always learn something, even when doing something you've done a few times! It consists of a Golang backend, utilizing GIN and GORM as router and ORM respectively. For persistance, I chose PostgreSQL, and Angular with PrimeNG and TailwindCSS on the frontend. For version control and CI/CD I am using Github/Github Actions and Kubernetes. Lastly, the application is running in the Hetzner Cloud.
